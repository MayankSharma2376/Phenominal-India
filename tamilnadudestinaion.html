Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is situated in southern part of India. It is bound by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka in the northern side, Indian Ocean in the southern side, Bay of Bengal in the eastern side and Kerala in the western side. Tamil Nadu is known for its chains of mountains, plateaus, residual hills and coastal plains. People of Tamil Nadu are popular for their taste in music, dance, literature, theatre, etc..

Place to Visit in Tamil Nadu

Ooty

Ooty, officially known as Udhagamandalam, is a charming hill station in Tamil Nadu, nestled in the Nilgiri Hills. Often called the "Queen of Hill Stations," Ooty is famous for its misty mountains, lush tea gardens, and colonial-era charm. KodaiKanal

Kodaikanal, often called the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a breathtaking retreat in Tamil Nadu, nestled in the Palani Hills of the Western Ghats. It sits at an altitude of 2,225 meters (7,300 feet) and is known for its misty landscapes, serene lakes, and lush forests.
