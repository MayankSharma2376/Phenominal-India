Tour of Kerala- God's Own Country.

Kerala, located on the tropical Malabar Coast of southwestern India, is known for its palm-lined beaches, backwaters, rich culture, and vibrant festivals. Its capital is Thiruvananthapuram.

-Mayank Sharma

Location

Situated on the southwestern tip of India, KL is bordered by Karnataka to the north and northeast, Tamil Nadu to the east and south, and the Arabian Sea on the west.

Fairs & Festivals

Onam is Kerala's most celebrated festival, a harvest festival marked by vibrant flower arrangements (Pookkalam), traditional dance, boat races, and the grand Onasadya feast. Other popular festivals include Vishu, Thrissur Pooram, and the Snake Boat Races in Alappuzha.

Hill Station in Tamil Nadu

Munnar
A popular hill station nestled in the Western Ghats, known for its tea plantations, misty hills, and wildlife sanctuaries.
Wayanad
Famous for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, caves, and wildlife. It offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by lush greenery.
Thekkady
Home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, Thekkady is known for its forests, spice plantations, and picturesque lake cruises.
