Tour of Haryana- The Land of Legends.

Haryana, a North Indian state surrounding the capital Delhi on three sides, is known for its rich history, agricultural heritage, and rapid urban development. The capital is Chandigarh (shared with Punjab).

-Mayank Sharma

Location

HR is located in Northern India. It is bordered by Punjab and Himachal Pradesh to the north, Rajasthan to the west and south, and Uttar Pradesh and Delhi to the east. The state encircles the national capital, making it strategically important.

Fairs & Festivals

Haryana celebrates various vibrant festivals. The **Surajkund Mela**, held in Faridabad, is an internationally renowned handicrafts fair. **Teej**, **Baisakhi**, and **Gugga Naumi** are widely celebrated with folk music and dance. Haryanvi culture is deeply reflected in these local traditions.

Hill Station in Haryana

Though Haryana is largely a plains state, it offers hilly getaways near the Shivalik foothills:

Morni Hills
The only hill station in Haryana, located near Panchkula. It's known for its scenic views, lakes, and trekking trails.
Kalesar Forest Hills
Located in the Yamunanagar district, this area offers a quiet natural retreat and wildlife experiences.
Panchkula Hills
On the border with Himachal, ideal for nature lovers and weekend getaways.
