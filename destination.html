Kerala

Kerala is situated on the southwestern tip of India. The state of Kerala is divided into hills and valleys, midland plains and coastal belts. The backwaters suitated along the coastal betls are one of the main tourist attraction of Kerala. Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is situated in southern part of India. It is bound by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka in the northern side, Indian Ocean in the southern side, Bay of Bengal in the eastern side and Kerala in the western side. Tamil Nadu is known for its chains of mountains, plateaus, residual hills and coastal plains. People of Tamil Nadu are popular for their taste in music, dance, literature, theatre, etc.. . Haryana

Haryana is a state in northern India, known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and strong agricultural base. It was carved out of Punjab in 1966 and has since become a hub for industry and technology, especially in cities like Gurugram and Faridabad. Haryana is also famous for its role in ancient Indian history, particularly as the land of Kurukshetra, where the epic battle of the Mahabharata took place. Read More..

Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh (UP) is India's most populous state, located in the northern part of the country. It has a rich historical and cultural heritage, with cities like Agra, home to the Taj Mahal, and Varanasi, one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities. The state capital is Lucknow, known for its Nawabi culture and architecture. UP is also a major agricultural and industrial hub, contributing significantly to India's economy. It hosts famous festivals like Kumbh Mela and has diverse landscapes, from the Gangetic plains to the Vindhya hills. Read More..
